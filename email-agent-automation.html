Common questions

How do crm_reply and formspree_lead views differ?

crm_reply is for OutreachEngine campaign responses; formspree_lead isolates website form intake; manual threads stay separate so operators never hunt one mixed Gmail inbox.

Which email providers work?

Gmail, Zoho, and Microsoft are supported with per-brand credential mapping for KL, KG, ST, and Faith Works lanes.

Is Email-Agent only for CRM?

No — it also routes Formspree and manual business threads. CRM reply management is one view among several.

How does bounce handling work?

Bounce and failure signals notify operators and feed back into OutreachEngine so bad addresses stop before the next send window.

Can I access it outside Knight Command?

Port 5100 is reachable directly; Knight Command embeds it for single-sign-on operator workflow.
