Case study breakdown

Demonstrates the CRM Outreach & Lead Generation service in production on Screen Team, Faith Works, and Knight Group lanes.

Problem

Manual outreach mixed templates, lists, and sender reputations across Knight Logics client brands. Replies scattered in personal Gmail. Follow-up timing was inconsistent and bounces poisoned future sends.

Without queue preview and brand isolation, a good campaign for one trade could accidentally burn another brand’s sender reputation.

  • No queue preview before sends
  • KG and ST campaigns risked cross-brand template mistakes
  • Follow-up depended on someone remembering inbox checks

What was built

OutreachEngine — Flask app with SQLite storage — drives segmented lists, branded templates, scheduler jobs for first_touch and followup, bounce detection, and daily caps between 20 and 40 sends per brand. Replies route to Email-Agent crm_reply views on port 5100.

Operators preview depth and caps before the scheduler fires, then review replies in a dedicated CRM path instead of personal inboxes.

  • Brand switcher for kl, kg, and st lanes
  • Preview and queue depth before scheduler fires
  • Bounce flags suppress bad addresses

Tools used

Flask + SQLite backend, custom CRM UI, scheduler integration, Email-Agent multi-inbox routing with KL/KG/ST mapping, and Knight Command Outreach CRM tab embed.

Campaign logging ties list → send → reply → booked work so lead-source reporting is not a spreadsheet reconstruction.

  • Gmail/Zoho/Microsoft inboxes via Email-Agent
  • Campaign logging for lead-source reporting
  • Embeds on port 5050 family alongside referral tooling where co-deployed

Early signal

Common questions

Is this a third-party CRM we resell?

No. OutreachEngine is Knight Logics production software configured per client brand. The case study describes the live workflow we operate and can adapt for your trade and territory.

How do you keep brands from mixing templates?

Each lane (kl, kg, st, faithworks, and others) has isolated templates, senders, and daily caps. Operators switch brands explicitly before preview or send.

What happens when someone replies?

Replies route into Email-Agent crm_reply views on port 5100 so follow-up is not trapped in a personal Gmail inbox.

Can you run this for my company without building a full admin shell?

Yes. We can deploy OutreachEngine for your lists and caps first, then embed it in Knight Command–style admin later if your team needs a unified ops hub.
