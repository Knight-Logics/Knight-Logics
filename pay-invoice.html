How invoice payment works

  • Enter the invoice number from your project agreement or invoice email.
  • Enter the amount shown on the invoice exactly as written.
  • Fill in your name and email — Stripe sends the receipt automatically.
  • You will be redirected to a secure Stripe checkout page to complete the payment.

What your invoice number looks like

  • Invoice numbers are listed at the top of your Knight Logics project agreement or invoice email.
  • Common formats: KL-2026-001, KL/WEB/042, or the reference number provided by Nicholas directly.
  • If you are not sure of your invoice number, check your email or reach out before paying.

Payment security

  • All payments are processed through Stripe — a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor.
  • Knight Logics never stores or sees your card information.
  • Stripe sends a receipt to the email you provide.
